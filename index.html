Intro

Hi, all! I am a PhD candidate in Astrophysics at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Andalucía (IAA-CSIC), based in Granada, Spain. I obtained my master's and bachelor's degrees in Physics from the Universidade Federal de Santa Maria, located in the same city where I was born, Santa Maria (Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil).

I am passionate about Astronomy and active galaxies in particular. My main lines of research are active galactic nuclei (AGN), quasars, and radiogalaxies. Briefly, during my PhD I am dedicated on the UV and optical spectral analysis of a sample of high-redshift and high-luminosity quasars with very peculiar characteristics and their location in the so-called Main Sequence of quasars.

Apart from this, I am also interested in Astronomy outreach and on making this amazing science accessible to everybody, specially for elementary and high school students.

Nice to see you here! Hope you enjoy! :D

Main collaborations:

- Gas phase metallicity determinations in nearby AGNs with SDSS-IV MaNGA: evidence of metal poor accretion (do Nascimento+22, MNRAS)

- Isolating an outflow component in single-epoch spectra of quasars (Marziani,Deconto-Machado,del Olmo+22, Galaxies)
